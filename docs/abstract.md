A Patient Registration and Electronic Medical Record (EMR) Portal is a simple digital system that helps hospitals
and clinics manage patient information more efficiently. Instead of using paper forms and files, the system allows
staff to register patients by entering their basic details like name, age, contact information, and appointment data. 
Each patient is given a unique ID, making it easy to find their information later. This reduces confusion, saves time 
at the reception, and keeps all patient data organized in one place.

The EMR part of the system focuses on storing the patient’s medical details in a clear and structured way.
Doctors can record diagnoses, prescriptions, and test reports, and can easily access a patient’s past records
during future visits. This helps in better decision-making and ensures that patients receive consistent care.
Overall, the portal makes healthcare management smoother, reduces paperwork, and improves the experience for
both patients and medical staff.
